A short film told through 12 songs.
One story. One night. One listen.

12:12 represents the intersection of twelve creative voices unified by a single vision. By weaving together a 12-track score with high-concept cinematography, we have crafted a visual album that serves as a deep, immersive exploration of human emotions. It is a curated journey through the complexities of the heart, designed for those who seek depth in every beat and resonance in every frame.
